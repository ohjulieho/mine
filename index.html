About me

The name is Julie Ho! This is the part where I speak shameless about myself and I promise to keep it to a minimal! After graduating from CSU Long Beach with an Industrial Design degree, I embarked on post-grad travels while taking on freelance design projects. I spent 2 years working as a UI & UX Designer in San Francisco for enterprise software.

While there, I learned that I enjoy distilling and organizing content into elegant pieces for others to enjoy and use. For example, one of my favorite projects was designing and developing the company's first style guide.

When I'm not nose-deep in pixels, you can find me enjoying the company of my friends and family or collecting stamps on my passport for my next adventure. I'm currently in my next phase of my career, so if you or someone you know is looking for a versatile designer to work with, let's get in touch!
